Why this works

A proven model, adapted for India.

Index-based insurance already runs at scale elsewhere \u2014 ACRE Africa has used weather-indexed policies to cover smallholder farmers across Kenya, Rwanda, and Tanzania for over a decade, and similar index instruments back sovereign disaster-risk pools globally.

The mechanism isn't experimental. What's missing in India is a marketplace that makes it easy for a farmer to find, compare, and buy the right policy for their crop, region, and sowing month \u2014 that's the gap FarmFuture fills.

An honest caveat

Satellite and weather-station data isn't uniformly fine-grained across every district in India yet. FarmFuture is the most viable path forward as ground-station density and satellite resolution keep improving \u2014 not a claim that it's pixel-perfect everywhere today.
